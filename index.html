About

The short version.

I'm a Senior UX Designer and Product Designer with over 15 years of experience designing and delivering enterprise SaaS platforms, webbase applications, Energy Trading & Risk Management (ETRM) systems, compliance platforms, and data-intensive business applications.

I specialize in transforming complex workflows into intuitive, scalable user experiences — through user-centered design, interaction design, workflow optimization, and design systems. I work closely with Product Managers, Engineers, and QA teams in Agile environments to deliver production-ready solutions, not just polished mockups.

Across multiple companies and a string of long-tenure roles, I've built a track record of shipping enterprise products used by global customers and improving usability for operational workflows that most designers never get to touch.
